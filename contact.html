Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your class timings?

We offer flexible timings from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. You can choose morning, afternoon, or evening batches based on your convenience.

Do you provide instruments for practice?

Yes, all our classrooms are equipped with high-quality instruments. However, for faster progress, we recommend students have their own instrument for home practice.

What is the minimum age requirement?

We accept students from age 5 onwards. We have specially designed programs for children (5-10 years), teenagers (11-17 years), and adults (18+ years).

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes. Our online classes are conducted via Zoom with the same quality of instruction as in-person sessions.

What is your fee payment policy?

Fees are payable monthly in advance. We accept payment via online transfer, UPI, credit/debit cards, and cash. Late payment may result in temporary suspension of classes.
