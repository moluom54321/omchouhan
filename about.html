About Us

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Welcome to Music School of Delhi

Founded in 2010, Music School of Delhi has been at the forefront of music education in the capital city for over 14 years. What started as a small studio with a handful of passionate students has grown into one of Delhi's most prestigious music institutions.

Our journey has been marked by dedication, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We've nurtured thousands of students, many of whom have gone on to win national and international competitions, perform on prestigious stages, and even pursue professional careers in music.

Our Mission

To provide world-class music education that empowers students to discover their musical potential, develop technical excellence, and express themselves creatively. We aim to make quality music education accessible to everyone, regardless of age or background.

Our Vision

To be recognized as India's leading music institution that transforms lives through music. We envision a future where every student who walks through our doors leaves not just as a skilled musician, but as a confident, creative individual ready to share their gift with the world.

Our Journey

14 Years of Excellence in Music Education

2010 - The Beginning

Music School of Delhi was founded with just 15 students and 2 instructors in a small studio in Pitampura, New Delhi.

2013 - First Achievement

Our students won their first national competition, putting our school on the map.

2016 - Expansion

Expanded to a larger facility with 10 practice rooms and state-of-the-art equipment.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched online classes and digital learning resources, reaching students across India.

2024 - Present Day

Now serving 500+ active students with 20+ professional instructors and a 95% success rate.
